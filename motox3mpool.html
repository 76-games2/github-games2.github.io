Moto X3M Pool Party : The Ultimate Water Bike Challenge

Dive into the thrilling world of Moto X3M Pool Party, the most exciting water based bike racing game online. This adrenaline pumping sequel to the popular Moto X3M series takes your stunt bike to splashy new environments filled with water obstacles, ramps, and crazy loops.

How to Play Moto X3M Pool Party:

  1. Use arrow keys or WASD to control your bike
  2. Press up arrow/W to accelerate
  3. Press down arrow/S to brake or flip backwards
  4. Lean forward/backward with left/right arrows (A/D) to maintain balance
  5. Complete all levels as fast as possible to earn 3 stars
  6. Watch out for water traps and perform awesome mid air stunts!

With its physics-based gameplay and watery challenges, Moto X3M Pool Party offers endless entertainment for stunt bike enthusiasts. The game features multiple levels with increasing difficulty, awesome water physics, and spectacular crashes!
