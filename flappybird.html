Flappy Bird is an addictive and simple game where you guide a small bird through a series of green pipes without crashing. The goal is to fly as far as you can without hitting any obstacles. It's easy to learn but hard to master!

How to Play Flappy Bird:

  • Click or tap the screen to make the bird flap its wings.
  • Time your taps to fly through the gaps between the pipes.
  • Don't hit the ground or the pipes – it’s game over!
  • Try to get the highest score possible.
