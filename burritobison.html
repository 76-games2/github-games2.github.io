Play Burrito Bison Online

Burrito Bison is a thrilling arcade style launch game that combines action, humor, and strategy. Launch yourself into the sky as the mighty Burrito Bison, bouncing off gummy bears and collecting coins while smashing through obstacles and unlocking upgrades. Perfect for fans of fast-paced and addictive gameplay!

How to Play Burrito Bison

  • Press the spacebar or click to launch Burrito Bison into the ring.
  • Bounce off gummy bears to keep your momentum going.
  • Use power-ups and upgrades to fly farther and faster.
  • Smash through bosses and unlock new characters to progress through the game.
  • Earn coins each run to buy powerful upgrades and improve your distance.
