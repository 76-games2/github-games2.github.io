Duck Life

Duck Life : Train, Compete, and Conquer!

Duck Life is an exciting adventure and training game where you raise a duckling into a true champion. Train it in various skills including running, swimming, flying, and climbing. Once your duck is strong enough, compete in races to prove you're the best!

How to Play Duck Life

  • Use the arrow keys to control your duck during training exercises.
  • Each training mode improves a specific skill (running, swimming, flying).
  • Earn coins during training to buy food or upgrades.
  • Enter races when your duck is ready – you’ll need to pass qualifications to compete!

Enjoy playing Duck Life directly on Github Games with no download required.
