The Chrome Dino game (also known as the No Internet Game) is a hidden gem in Google Chrome that appears when you lose internet connection. This simple yet addictive endless runner features a T-Rex dinosaur that jumps over cacti and ducks under obstacles.

How to Play Chrome Dino Game

  1. Disconnect from the internet or visit chrome://dino in your browser
  2. Press Spacebar or Up Arrow to start the game
  3. Press Spacebar/Up Arrow to jump over cacti
  4. Press Down Arrow to duck under flying pterodactyls
  5. Try to survive as long as possible - the game speeds up over time!
