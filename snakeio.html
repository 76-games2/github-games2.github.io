Enjoy the classic Snake Google game right in your browser. This addictive game lets you guide a growing snake to collect apples while avoiding running into walls or itself. How to Play Snake Google

  • Use the arrow keys (↑, ↓, ←, →) to control the snake’s direction.
  • Eat the red dots (apples) to grow longer.
  • Avoid hitting the snake's body or the wall — or it’s game over!
  • The longer you survive, the higher your score!
