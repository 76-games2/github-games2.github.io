How to Play Basketball Legends

  • Single Player: Use WASD to move and jump, X to shoot/steal, and Z to super shot.
  • Two Players:
    • Player 1: WASD to move, B to shoot/steal, V for super shot.
    • Player 2: Arrow keys to move, L to shoot/steal, K for super shot.
  • Score more points than your opponent before the timer runs out to win the match.
