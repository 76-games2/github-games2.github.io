Play Google Feud Online

Google Feud is a fun and addictive trivia game based on Google’s autocomplete feature. Your goal is to guess the top autocomplete suggestions for a given phrase. It's a perfect mix of humor, logic, and popular culture knowledge.

How to Play Google Feud

  1. Choose a category: Culture, People, Names, or Questions.
  2. You’ll see a partial phrase or question.
  3. Try to guess how Google would autocomplete it.
  4. You get three chances to guess the top results correctly.

Each correct guess earns you points based on its rank. The higher the suggestion on Google's list, the more points you score!
