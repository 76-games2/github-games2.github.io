Soccer Random is a hilarious and unpredictable 2-player football game where chaos meets fun. From icy fields to beach sand, every round throws a new twist your way. Experience crazy kicks, flipping players, and physics-based madness in each match. Whether you play solo or with a friend, every moment in Soccer Random is entertaining and challenging!

How to Play Soccer Random

  • Single Player Mode: Use the Up Arrow key to jump and kick.
  • Two Player Mode:
    • Player 1: Press W to jump/kick.
    • Player 2: Press Up Arrow to jump/kick.
  • Score goals by kicking the ball into your opponent’s net.
  • First to score 5 goals wins the match!
