Fireboy and Watergirl is a legendary two-player puzzle-platform game that challenges you to control both characters through obstacle-filled temples. Use teamwork to solve puzzles, trigger levers, and avoid deadly traps to escape each level successfully.

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl

  • Fireboy: Use the Arrow Keys to move and jump. Avoid water pools!
  • Watergirl: Use A, W, D keys to move. Stay away from lava!
  • Step on pressure plates, move boxes, and cooperate to open doors and reach the exit together.
  • Collect all diamonds for a perfect score in each level.
