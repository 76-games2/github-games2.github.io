How to Play Getaway Shootout

  • Move: Use the W and E keys (Player 1) or I and O keys (Player 2) to jump left or right.
  • Power-Up: Use R (Player 1) or P (Player 2) to use weapons or items.
  • Collect weapons and bonuses along the way to gain an advantage.
  • First to reach the getaway point wins the round!
