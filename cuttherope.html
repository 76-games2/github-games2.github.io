Play Cut the Rope Online

Dive into the world of Cut the Rope, the award winning physics based puzzle game where your mission is simple yet addictive — feed the adorable monster Om Nom by cutting ropes and collecting stars! With intuitive controls and challenging levels, this game tests your timing and logic skills at every stage.

How to Play Cut the Rope

  • Use your mouse or finger to cut the ropes holding the candy.
  • Time your cuts carefully to swing or drop the candy into Om Nom’s mouth.
  • Collect stars for bonus points on each level.
  • Avoid obstacles like spikes and bubbles that may interfere with your goal.
