How to Play 2048 Cupcakes

  • Use your arrow keys (↑ ↓ ← →) or swipe on mobile to move the cupcakes around the grid.
  • When two identical cupcakes touch, they merge into a new, more delicious cupcake!
  • Your goal is to reach the final cupcake by merging matching tiles.
  • Plan your moves strategically—if the grid fills up with no possible moves, the game is over.
