Just Fall is an exciting multiplayer game where you control a cute penguin and try to be the last one standing on slippery hexagonal tiles. Whether you're playing solo or with friends, Just Fall is all about fast reflexes and strategic movement.

How to Play Just Fall

  • Use W, A, S, D to move your penguin.
  • Press the spacebar to jump.
  • Move across platforms and avoid falling off the disappearing tiles.
  • The goal is to stay on the platform longer than other players.
  • Watch your step – tiles vanish quickly!

The longer you survive, the higher your score. Be agile and plan your path wisely to outlast your opponents. It's a test of timing, strategy, and cool-headed movement.
