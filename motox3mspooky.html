Moto X3M Spooky: A Spine Chilling Bike Adventure

Moto X3M Spooky is the Halloween themed version of the adrenaline pumping bike racing game. With haunted tracks, creepy obstacles, and a ghostly atmosphere, this edition delivers frightful fun for players of all ages!

How to Play Moto X3M Spooky:

  1. Accelerate using the UP arrow or W key.
  2. Lean forward/backward with the DOWN arrow or S key to balance.
  3. Navigate through spooky obstacles like tombstones, ghosts, and pumpkins.
  4. Complete each level as fast as possible to earn a 3-star rating!
  5. Watch out for deadly traps one wrong move could end your race!
