Clicker Heroes is one of the most addictive idle games ever created. In this incremental RPG, your goal is to click on monsters to defeat them and collect gold. Use the gold to hire and upgrade powerful heroes who will automatically do damage for you even when you're not actively playing.

FAQs about Clicker Heroes

Is Clicker Heroes free to play?

Yes, you can play Clicker Heroes completely free in your browser without downloading anything.

Do I need to be online to progress?

No, your heroes continue to fight and earn gold even when you're offline!

Can I save my progress?

Yes, use the in-game save feature or sync with cloud storage to keep your progress safe.
