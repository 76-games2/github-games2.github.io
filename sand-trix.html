Sand Tetris: A Fresh Twist on a Classic Puzzle Game

Sand Tetris reinvents the beloved block-stacking game with physics-based gameplay where pieces behave like real sand particles. This innovative take on the classic Tetris formula challenges players to think differently about gravity and block placement.

How to Play Sand Tetris

  1. Use arrow keys to move falling sand blocks left and right
  2. Press the up arrow to rotate blocks
  3. The down arrow accelerates the block's descent
  4. Create complete horizontal lines to make them disappear
  5. Watch out! Sand particles may shift after placement
  6. The game ends when blocks stack to the top of the play area

Sand Tetris FAQ

What makes Sand Tetris different from regular Tetris?
Sand Tetris introduces realistic physics where blocks settle like sand particles, creating unpredictable patterns.
Is Sand Tetris available on mobile?
Yes! The mobile version features touch controls optimized for smartphones.
Can I compete with other players?
Sand Tetris offers both single-player and multiplayer battle modes.
