Welcome to Block Breaker, the classic arcade game where you smash blocks, collect power-ups, and rack up points! Whether you're a retro gaming fan or just looking to kill time, Block Breaker offers fast-paced, brick-smashing action right in your browser.

How to Play Block Breaker

  • Use your mouse or arrow keys to move the paddle left and right.
  • Click or press spacebar to launch the ball.
  • Break all the blocks by bouncing the ball off the paddle.
  • Catch falling power-ups to enhance your paddle or multiply balls.
  • Don't let the ball fall below the paddle — or you’ll lose a life!

As you advance, levels become more challenging with new layouts and faster speeds. Block Breaker is a great way to sharpen your reflexes and enjoy nostalgic fun.
