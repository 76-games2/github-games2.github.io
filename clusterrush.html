How to Play Cluster Rush

  • Use W, A, S, D or Arrow Keys to move.
  • Press Spacebar to jump.
  • Land on moving trucks and avoid falling off.
  • Dodge obstacles and time your jumps precisely.
  • Stay on the trucks to survive and reach the goal.
